## Domain Options
You can apply for any of the following domains during the auditions. Each domain offers unique responsibilities and learning experiences, allowing you to grow in your area of interest.

### 1. Technical Team
Role: You will help us to plan and deliver technical sessions, workshops, coding activities and share knowledge on programming, data structures, cloud technologies, and other tech-related topics.
Preparation Tip: Have a basic understanding of programming basics, data structures, or any specific tech skills you're passionate about. For technical roles, practice problem-solving, basic coding skills, or any project you’ve worked on.

### 2. Graphic Designing Team
Role: Create visually appealing designs for posters, social media posts, banners, and event materials. Enhance the visual identity of the club.
Preparation Tip: Familiarize yourself with design tools like Canva, Adobe Photoshop, or Illustrator. Be ready to show some of your previous design work.

### 3. Creative Team (Video Editing, Photography)
Role: Capture event highlights through photos and videos. Edit videos for social media, promo videos, and event recaps.
Preparation Tip: Practice using video editing software such as Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro, or simpler tools like InShot. If you're into photography, brush up on composition and camera techniques.

### 4. Event Management Team
Role: Assist in planning, organizing, and executing various events like workshops, hackathons, and tech talks. Involved in event logistics, registrations, and scheduling.
Preparation Tip: Be organized and proactive. Prior experience in organizing events or handling logistics will be beneficial.

### 5. Social Media Team
Role: Manage the club's social media presence by creating and scheduling posts, stories, and updates. Engage with the audience and promote events to boost online visibility.
Preparation Tip: Get familiar with different social media platforms like Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn. Think of creative strategies to attract more followers and increase engagement.

### 6. Editorial Team
Role: Write and edit content for newsletters, blogs, event descriptions, and social media captions. Help communicate the club's initiatives and achievements effectively.
Preparation Tip: Showcase strong writing skills, and be prepared to share writing samples such as articles, blog posts, or previous content you've worked on.

### 7. PR/Marketing Team
Role: Promote the club’s activities and events both online and offline. Work on building partnerships, collaborating with sponsors, and increasing brand visibility.
Preparation Tip: Have good communication skills and a knack for persuasion. Experience in PR or marketing, even on a small scale, will be a plus.
